Color Usage Guidelines

Text & Typography

  • Headings: Deep Brown (#4a3528)
  • Body text: Warm Brown (#6b4e3d)
  • Links: Warm Brown with Gold hover
  • Buttons: Deep Brown background

Backgrounds

  • Main: Warm Cream (#faf9f7)
  • Sections: Light Beige (#f7f3ed)
  • Cards: Cream Accents (#f5f1ed)
  • Contact: Soft Beige (#f2ebe3)

Accents & Details

  • Premium touches: Muted Gold (#c4a373)
  • Borders: Warm Stone (#e8ddd4)
  • Icons: Gold or Deep Brown
  • Hover states: Gold accents

Brand Consistency

  • Logo matches: Logo Beige (#d4c4b0)
